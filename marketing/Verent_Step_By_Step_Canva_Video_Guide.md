# Verent Step-By-Step Canva Video Guide

Follow this file from top to bottom.
This is the cleanest way to create your first professional Verent promo video.

This guide is for:
- 1 main video
- Vertical mobile format
- 60 seconds
- Edited in Canva Pro
- Recorded on iPhone only

You are the hands.
I am giving you the exact sequence.

## 1. Final Goal

Create one premium 9:16 promo video that:
- looks clean and modern
- shows both renter and lister flows
- shows trust with wallet and on-chain proof
- feels like a real startup promo, not a random screen recording

## 2. Exact Style To Use

Use these settings everywhere in the video.

### Format
- Size: `1080 x 1920`
- Ratio: `9:16`
- Length: `60 seconds`
- Export: `MP4`

### Fonts
- Main headline font: `Montserrat Bold`
- Small text / subtitle / label font: `Open Sans`

If `Montserrat Bold` is not available, use `Poppins Bold`.
If `Open Sans` is not available, use `Arial`.

### Colors
- Main dark: `#0A0A0A`
- Main white: `#FFFFFF`
- Verent green: `#2ECC71`
- Soft gray text: `#6B7280`
- Light background: `#F9FAFB`

### Text Rules
- Never put too much text on the screen
- Maximum 1 key text at a time
- Maximum 2 lines
- Keep text short and premium
- Keep text inside the center safe area

### Motion Rules
- Use only `Dissolve` or `Slide`
- Transition speed: `0.2s` to `0.4s`
- Do not use flashy animations
- Do not zoom too much
- The app UI should stay readable

## 3. Before You Record On iPhone

Do this first.

1. Turn `Do Not Disturb` ON.
2. Set brightness around `75%`.
3. Clean your screen.
4. Keep text size at normal size.
5. Record in portrait only.
6. Turn microphone OFF for screen recordings.
7. Record each clip slowly and cleanly.
8. Do not tap too fast.
9. Hold 1 second before the first tap.
10. Hold 1 second before ending each clip.

Important:
- Record every clip a little slower than normal.
- This makes the final video feel more premium.

   - `#0A0A0A`

   - `#2ECC71`
   - `#6B7280`
6. Set fonts:
   - Headline = `Montserrat Bold`

## 7. Voiceover Recording Instructions

Do this after the video timeline is roughly ready.

### Best method
Record voiceover in a quiet room using:
- iPhone Voice Memos
- or Canva voice recording

### Voice style
- calm
- premium
- not too excited
- not too slow
- speak clearly

### Rule
If one sentence sounds too long, speak a little slower and give small pauses.
Do not rush.

## 8. Music Instructions

Use a modern tech/startup background music.

### Music should feel
- clean
- premium
- modern
- light energy
- not cinematic and dramatic

### Music volume
- Voiceover = main
- Music = low
- Music should never fight with the voice

### SFX
If needed, add only:
- 1 soft tap sound
- 1 soft whoosh
- 1 soft transition sound

Do not overdo sound effects.
