# QA + A/B Test Pack

## Pre-Publish QA Checklist

Run this before exporting each version.

- Hook message appears in first 2 seconds.
- Text is readable on a 6-inch phone screen.
- No scene has more than one key message.
- Renter and lister scenes are clearly separated.
- Wallet and on-chain proof scenes are included.
- CTA is visible for at least 3 seconds.
- Voiceover is always louder than background music.
- No frame shows notifications or private personal data.

## A/B Hook Variants (for 60s Social)

### Hook A - Pain First
- On-screen: `Device rentals are growing. Trust is not.`
- Voiceover: `Device rentals are growing fast, but trust is still broken.`

### Hook B - Product First
- On-screen: `Meet the mobile-first rental platform.`
- Voiceover: `Meet Verent, a mobile-first platform for trusted device rentals.`

## Posting Test Protocol

- Publish Hook A and Hook B within the same day window.
- Keep caption, hashtags, and posting time as close as possible.
- Track after 24-48 hours:
  - 3-second hold rate
  - 50% completion rate
  - CTA click-through
- Winner becomes the default hook for the next campaign.
